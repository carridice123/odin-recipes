Pine Apple Sangria

Recipe Description

At first glance this may lood like any other home made pine apple juice right?
See that's where your wrong. Although this beverage isn't difficult to make its completely different from the homemade one.
That's because of this recipe here. It combine different types of citruses together However its the amount of each citrus used to make ther beverage here thats important. pay attention to the instruction for best results.
Oh and this beverage tastes better when cooled.

Ingredients

  • 2 (750 milliliter) bottles Sauvignon Blanc
  • 2 cups pineapple juice
  • 1 cup triple sec
  • ½ cup brandy
  • ½ cup chopped fresh pineapple
  • ½ cup chopped orange
  • 1 lemon, seeded and chopped
  • ¼ cup chopped lime
  • 2 cups lemon-lime flavored carbonated beverage

Steps

  1. Mix Sauvignon Blanc, pineapple juice, triple sec, and brandy together in a large pitcher. Add chopped pineapple, orange, lemon, and lime. Cover and refrigerate, 8 hours to overnight.
  2. Stir in lemon-lime soda before serving.
